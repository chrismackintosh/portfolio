Design systems aren’t just about buttons and components.  
They’re agreements. Agreements that keep teams aligned, help scale accessibility, and ensure every resident or customer gets a consistent, trustworthy experience.

I’ve seen firsthand how clear tokens + semantic structure can save hundreds of engineering hours — and more importantly, build user trust.
