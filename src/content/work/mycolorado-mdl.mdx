## TL;DR

A new digital identity experience integrated into the myColorado app.  
My role: led design system extension, accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.2 AA), and TSA-ready credential flow.

---

### Problem

Residents needed a secure, accessible, and standards-compliant digital driver license.

### Approach

- Extended the existing design system with identity-specific components.
- Collaborated with devs to ensure ISO/IEC 18013-5 compliance.
- Ran usability testing on TSA passenger flows.

### Outcome

- Increased onboarding completion by 23%.
- Reduced ID-related support tickets by 31%.
- Established reusable identity patterns in the design system.
